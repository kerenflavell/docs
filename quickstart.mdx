### Get started in under five minutes

1. **Create an Assistant** for general tasks.
2. **Open Chat** and send your first prompt.
3. **Launch AutoThink Canvas**, drop your Assistant onto the canvas, connect a few nodes.
4. **Experiment** with different roles and thinking strategies.
